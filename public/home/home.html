Problem

Within the Film Industry, there are very few avenues of entering into the industry full time. Almost every filmmaker attempts to join the industry because they have a Story to tell, or they want to help others tell theirs. But the majority never make it into the industry, therefore those Stories are never told to the World.

group

Solution

By locating other Story-Tellers around you, we provide you with the opportunity to combine talents and ideas to create the Untold Stories you have dreamt of. We compile the team and give you the keys to create without the hassle of a Studio or a Producer.

settings

Result

Everyone has the opportunity to create the way they want to. Whether you are a Director, Writer, Boom Operator, or you simply want to Design Costumes, you now have the ability to do so. So, the Ultimate Result is unleashing the plethora of Untold Stories upon the World. And in turn, making the world a better place for everyone.
